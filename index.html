Global Metro Expansion (1990–2020)

This map shows metro system length growth in major global cities (1990–2020). Circle size represents metro length (km). A small dot shows each city center point. Use the slider and step buttons on the map to explore change over time. Click a city to retrieve details (city + country + current-year value).

Discussion Prompt

This map visualizes the expansion of metro systems in major global cities between 1990 and 2020.

While some cities have long-established networks that expanded gradually, others experienced rapid metro development during recent decades.

What factors might explain these different trajectories of urban transit development?

Consider historical infrastructure, population growth, government investment strategies, and environmental or mobility policies.
